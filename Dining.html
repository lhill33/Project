The Gimme Dining Experince

Indulge in a culinary experience that matches the elegance of our golf facilities. Whether it’s a casual post-game snack, a gourmet dinner, or a celebratory meal, our dining options offer something for every palate. With a menu crafted from the freshest ingredients, our on-site restaurant ensures that your time off the course is just as enjoyable as your time on it.

Breakfast

Whether you're fueling up before your round or enjoying a relaxed morning, our breakfast menu offers a variety of options to suit every taste. From freshly baked pastries and wholesome yogurt parfaits to classic favorites like fluffy pancakes, eggs any style, and hearty breakfast burritos, there’s something to please every palate. Pair your meal with a freshly brewed cup of coffee or a refreshing smoothie as you take in the beautiful morning views of the course.

Lunch

Our lunch menu features a wide range of options perfect for a quick bite or a leisurely midday meal. From gourmet sandwiches and crisp salads to delicious wraps and signature burgers, there's something to suit every craving. If you're in the mood for something lighter, try one of our fresh, seasonal salads, or indulge in one of our chef’s daily specials. With indoor and outdoor seating available, you can relax and recharge while enjoying the stunning views of the course.

Dinner

As the sun sets, our dining options shift to a more refined menu, perfect for an evening of relaxation. Our dinner menu features a selection of mouthwatering steaks, fresh seafood, and flavorful pasta dishes, all prepared with the finest ingredients. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet dinner for two or celebrating with friends, our extensive wine list and signature cocktails provide the perfect complement to your meal. The atmosphere is both inviting and elegant, making it the perfect spot to unwind after a great day on the course.
